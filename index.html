NIKE HISTORY TIMELINE

1964

Blue Ribbon Sports

Nike began as Blue Ribbon Sports, founded by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, selling Onitsuka Tiger shoes from Japan.

1971

The Nike Brand

The company officially became Nike, Inc. The famous Swoosh logo was created and the first Nike shoes were released.

1984

Air Jordan

Nike launched the Air Jordan line with basketball legend Michael Jordan, becoming a cultural icon worldwide.

1996

Global Expansion

Nike grew into a global leader in sportswear, sponsoring top athletes and dominating the athletic footwear market.

Today

Innovation & Sustainability

Nike continues to lead with innovation, performance gear, and sustainability efforts, inspiring athletes worldwide.
